About Driftless Health

At Driftless Health, we combine
traditional medicine with whole-person care

We combine evidence-based primary care with lifestyle medicine, health coaching, and integrative tools to deliver accurate understanding, effective treatment planning, and lasting health outcomes.

This practice was built for people who want more — more time with their provider, more nuance in their care, and more connection to the community they live in. The Driftless Region isn't just where we practice. It's home.

Whether you're managing a chronic condition, working toward prevention, or simply looking for a provider who will actually listen — Driftless Health was built for you.
